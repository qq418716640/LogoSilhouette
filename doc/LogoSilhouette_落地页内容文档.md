## 8. FAQ（SEO 优化版）

> 本 FAQ 用于提升搜索覆盖（long-tail keywords）与页面停留时间，建议同时生成 FAQ Schema（JSON-LD）。

### Q1. What is LogoSilhouette?
**LogoSilhouette** is a free online tool that converts logo images into clean black silhouette graphics.  
You can export the result as **SVG**, **transparent PNG**, or **white-background JPG**.

---

### Q2. Is LogoSilhouette free to use?
Yes. LogoSilhouette is completely free and does not require signup or login.

---

### Q3. Are my logo images uploaded to a server?
No. All image processing happens **locally in your browser**.  
Your files are never uploaded or stored on our servers.

---

### Q4. What image formats are supported?
You can upload **PNG, JPG, or WebP** images.  
For best results, use logos with clear contrast between the foreground and background.

---

### Q5. What export formats does LogoSilhouette support?
LogoSilhouette supports:
- **SVG** (vector format, ideal for design tools)
- **PNG** (transparent background)
- **JPG** (white background)

---

### Q6. What export sizes are available?
PNG and JPG exports are available in:
- 512 × 512
- **1024 × 1024 (recommended)**
- 2048 × 2048

SVG exports are resolution-independent.

---

### Q7. What is the default preset and who is it for?
The default preset is **Minimal Logo**, optimized for:
- Logo marks
- Icons
- Laser cutting and engraving
- Clean, simplified vector shapes

---

### Q8. Why does my silhouette look rough or noisy?
This usually happens when the source image has low contrast or too much background detail.  
Try:
- Using the **Minimal Logo** or **Clean Silhouette** preset  
- Increasing the simplification level  
- Uploading a higher-contrast logo image

---

### Q9. Can I edit the exported SVG file?
Yes. The exported SVG works in popular design tools such as **Figma**, **Adobe Illustrator**, and **Inkscape**.

---

### Q10. Is LogoSilhouette suitable for laser cutting or engraving?
Yes. The **Minimal Logo** preset produces simplified shapes with fewer vector nodes, making it suitable for laser cutting and engraving workflows.
